Us & Our Trainers

We've been going for 6 years now, and love helping people on their journeys just as much as we did the day we started. We're a team of 5 trainers who are all as enthusiastic and dedicated to their craft as each other. Each trainer will give you 100% commitment to whatever your needs and requirements are. Have a look below to get to know our Personal Trainers and to find out what areas they specialise in.

Meet the Trainers

Sophie

Sophie borrows from a range of disciplines including yoga, Pilates, conditioning, cardio and resistance bands to get results. Sophie takes the time to conduct a detailed assessment of lifestyle factors which can often have a significant effect on results. She considers stress, sleep, and nutrition meaning her clients get a holistic approach to their strength and fitness. These lifestyle factors are often the missing piece of the jigsaw, and taking the time to have a full plan in place means these results are outstanding.

James

James trains his clients using a blend of strength workouts, conditioning and boxing specific drills. James is listed as one of the best personal trainers in London because he uses a full range of weightlifting, bodyweight training, kettlebells and TRX to deliver sessions both in the gym, over Zoom and even across parks all over Kingston.

Emma

With 5 years experience Emma has trained people with a variety of goals including marathon training, weight loss injury recovery amongst others. Emma specislises in HIIT workouts, postural assesment, diet advice and functional training. She also keeps up to date with modern training methods and also likes to learn about old methods to widen her scope for helping people.

Tom

Tom studied Sport and Exercise Science at university, where he specialised in Strength, Power and Endurance training. He is keen to work with people at each and every level or fitness to help them improve, acheive their goals or to simply get them started.

Sally

Yoga teacher with over 10 years of experience offering fluid and dynamic classes to improve physical and emotional wellbeing. Offering Traditional Kundalini Yoga, Meditation, Yoga Nidra, Breath Work, Yin & Hatha Yoga Classes, Group & Private Lessons for all Ages & Abilities. London, Oxfordshire and online.
